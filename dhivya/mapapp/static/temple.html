Arulmigu Sri Pandi Muneeswaran Temple

The Arulmigu Sri Pandi Muneeswaran Temple is an ancient temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, known for its unique origin story and powerful deity, Pandi Muneeswarar. According to local legend, the deity is believed to be the ancient Pandya king Nedunchezhiyan, who is said to have been meditating as a penance for his role in the injustice done to Kovalan, the husband of Kannagi. The temple is located in the Melamadai area and is a significant spiritual site for those seeking divine justice and protection.
